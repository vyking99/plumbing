How to clear a clogged sink?

Clogged sinks are annoying because they slow down your morning routine and prevent you from getting your tasks completed on time. If left unattended, a clogged sink can become a major problem that will affect your daily routine. It can be really frustrating when you have guests over the house, but it’s always a nightmare for the homeowners. So, it’s not surprising if you are wondering “how do I clear a clogged sink?”. There are several ways through which you can resolve this issue. Before we start with the solution, let’s discuss why a sink gets clogged in the first place?

Why does a sink get clogged?

There are various reasons why sinks get clogged and it can be anything from hair, food debris, insects, and even pet waste. Most of the time, kitchen sinks are clogged because of the food waste that we leave behind after cooking. Food scraps can easily get trapped in the pipe and cause a clog. If you don’t dispose food waste properly, it can build up and cause a clog. Another common reason why sinks get clogged is because of grease build-up. Grease build-up can occur when you use cooking oil, butter, or other oils. If you do not clear the oil build-up from your kitchen sink, it can get trapped in the pipes and cause a clog. This is why it is very important to clear the sink on a regular basis to avoid these problems. Let’s take a look at how you can clear a kitchen sink clogged with food waste.

Step #1 – Check the pipes for build-up

The first thing you need to do is check the pipes for a build-up. If you can see a build-up in the pipes, it will be much easier for you to clear the clog. However, if you cannot see any build-up, you will need to look for alternative methods to clear the clog.To check for a build-up, you will need to turn off the water supply to the sink. Once the water is off, you can use a pipe cleaner or a shovel to check the pipes. If you can see any build-up, you can use a pipe cleaner to clear it out. If you cannot see any build-up, it will be best to turn off the water supply and then use a long shovel to clear the clog.

Step #2 – Clear the clog with a plunger

If you cannot see a build-up inside the pipes, you can use a plunger to clear the clog. You can use a plunger to push the clog out of the pipes and into the sink. You can push the clog into the trap or the plug that is connected to the sink.If you cannot push the clog out of the pipes, you can try using a plunger with a snake inside it. You can push the plunger into the sink and use the snake to push out the clog. Make sure to use gloves when you are using the plunger as it can cause serious injuries.

Step #3 – Try a pipe cleaner

If you cannot see a build-up in the pipes and you cannot push the clog out, you can try using a pipe cleaner to clear the clog. You can push the pipe cleaner into the clog and gently move it up and down. You can also try rotating the cleaner to break the clog. Make sure to push the cleaner slowly, because if you try to force it through the clog, it can cause a break in the pipes.Pipe cleaners work best when you use them to clear out the clog from the trap or the plug. You can try to push the cleaner through the trap or the plug, but it can be tricky. Make sure to use a pipe cleaner with a brush on one end and a scraper on the other end.
